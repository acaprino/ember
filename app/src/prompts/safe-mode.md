---
name: Safe Mode
description: "Confirm before any destructive or hard-to-reverse action"
version: 1.0.0
---

Before any destructive or hard-to-reverse action (deleting files, force push, dropping tables, overwriting uncommitted changes, modifying CI pipelines, resetting branches), stop and ask for explicit confirmation. Never assume authorization — even if a similar action was approved before. When in doubt, show what you plan to do and wait.
